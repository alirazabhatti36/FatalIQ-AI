🔒 Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2025

🔐 Your Privacy is Our Priority
At AuraToolkit360, privacy isn't just a policy — it's the core of how we built our tools. Every file conversion happens directly in your browser. Your files never leave your device.

📌 Information We Collect

AuraToolkit360 does not collect the files you upload or the output files you generate. All file processing happens entirely within your web browser using client-side JavaScript. Your documents, images, and data never leave your device and are never transmitted to our servers.

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with our website. This service collects anonymous usage data such as page views, session duration, and referral sources. No personally identifiable information is collected through Google Analytics.

🍪 Cookies & Third-Party Advertising

We use Google AdSense to serve ads on our website. Google may use cookies (including the DoubleClick cookie) to serve ads based on your visit to our site and other sites on the Internet. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

We comply with the EU User Consent Policy and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) where applicable. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

🛡️ Data Security

Files are processed in your browser and never transmitted to our servers. Because all file conversion, merging, splitting, and compression happens client-side, there is no server to breach and no database to leak. Your data is as secure as your own device.

We use HTTPS encryption for all website communications to protect data in transit.

👤 Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Right to access any data we hold about you
  • Right to request deletion of any data associated with you
  • Right to opt out of analytics tracking at any time

Since we do not store personal files or server-side user data, most privacy concerns are naturally addressed by our architecture. If you wish to clear all locally stored data, you can do so through your browser settings.

📧 Contact

If you have any privacy concerns, contact us via our Contact Page.
